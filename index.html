We move what is inside the shot.

              
Most generative video hands you one take and no notes. You get a look, not a scene. Change your mind about the car in the background and you regenerate everything, including the parts that were already right.

              
Découpage works the other way around. A frame is not a flat image to us. It is a set of objects and subjects we can address one at a time.

              
Move the actor, hold the room still. Swap the product, keep the light. Re-block the camera without recasting the face. That is the whole company.

              
            

          


          
          

            

            

              
Four axes of control.

              

                

                  
Subject

                  
Keep one face, one body, one performance across every shot. Re-direct the motion without recasting.

                

                

                  
Object

                  
Add, remove, or replace anything in the frame. The rest of the shot does not move.

                

                

                  
Camera

                  
Re-block the move after the take. Push in, orbit, or hold, on footage that already exists.

                

                

                  
Continuity

                  
Shot 4 remembers shot 1. Wardrobe, set dressing, and light carry across the cut.
